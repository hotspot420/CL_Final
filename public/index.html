Your Character

To build your character, answer at least 21 of the following questions. The more questions you answer, the more your character will resemble you. This website does not store any data after you exit the page. If you leave the page, your character will be lost and you will need to answer the questions again.

Once you've answered the questions, you will find yourself in a house with any number of other people, or you may be alone. The house will change if other players come and go. It responds to all of you, and also to you alone. You will receive more instructions after you've submitted your questions.
